LittleLoop — full app UI/UX
Parent-approved videos only · iOS-first · 23 hi-fi screens
Expert-panel consensus
Two visually distinct worlds in one system: Parent Mode — cream background, white cards, dashboard density, sky-blue CTAs; Child Mode — same palette but warmer, one-column oversized cards, zero chrome, coral accents. Every exit from Child Mode passes a PIN gate. The child never sees search, tabs, links, or settings. Paywall is calm, benefit-led, yearly-anchored.
Assumptions
Nunito stands in for SF Pro Rounded. Video thumbnails are neutral placeholders — no platform branding. Sample child “Emma”, bear avatar (both tweakable). Screens are numbered s01–s20 — reference them by number in chat.
